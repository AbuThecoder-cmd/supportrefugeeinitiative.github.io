Why Your Support Matters

In the face of adversity, refugees and displaced persons are often left without the basic necessities of
life. They face uncertain futures, lacking the stability and support needed to rebuild their lives.
SRI stands as a beacon of hope, working relentlessly to bridge the gap between despair and opportunity.
But we cannot do it alone. We need your help to make a profound impact.

Be the Change: Support Refugees Initiative (SRI)

Imagine a world where every voice is heard, every right is respected, and every person has the
chance to thrive.

Are you passionate about creating a better future for refugees and internally displaced persons?

At the Support Refugees Initiative (SRI), we're on a mission to ensure that the voices of refugees are
heard, their rights are respected, and they have access to essential social services. Our vision is a
world where every individual, whether a refugee or a national, can aspire to a brighter, more secure
future.

What We Do:

  • Advocacy & Protection
  • Education
  • Livelihoods
  • Nutrition & Medical Care
  • WASH (Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene)
  • Psychosocial Support

Support Refugees Initiative

Ensuring Peace of Mind

Creating stability and security for refugees through safe housing, legal assistance, and mental health support.

Creating Assimilation

Building bridges between refugees and nationals with language classes, cultural exchanges, and community engagement.

Strengthening Livelihoods

Empowering refugees economically through vocational training, microfinance opportunities, and job placement services.

Ending Gender-Based Violence

Providing safe spaces, counseling, and legal support to eradicate GBV and promote gender equality among refugees.

Nurturing the Next Generation

Investing in the future of refugee children through quality education, psychosocial care, and extracurricular activities.

Protecting Humanity

Advocating for refugee rights and ensuring every individual is treated with dignity and respect in times of crisis.

A Journey from Despair to Hope

"When my family and I fled our home, we had nothing but the clothes on our backs and a desperate
hope for survival. The Support Refugees Initiative welcomed us with open arms. They provided us with
food, medical care, and a safe place to stay. But more than that, they gave us hope.
Through their vocational training programs, I was able to learn new skills and secure a job
Today, I’m not just surviving; I’m thriving. My children are in school, and our future looks brighter than ever
SRI has truly transformed our lives, and for that, I am forever grateful" .

Rebuilding Lives, One Step at a Time

"Escaping the violence in our home country was only the beginning of our challenges. We arrived at the
refugee camp with no idea of what the future held. Thanks to SRI, my family received not only the
basics like food and clothing but also the emotional and psychological support we so desperately
needed. Their counseling services helped us heal and regain our strength. With their assistance, my
husband found work, and I started a small business through their microfinance program. Today, we are
rebuilding our lives, and I can’t express how much SRI has done for us."

From a Refugee to a Community Leader

"I will never forget the day I arrived at the refugee camp, scared and unsure of what the future would
bring. The Support Refugees Initiative was there for me from the start. They provided me with the
medical care I needed and, over time, involved me in their sensitization programs. Through these
programs, I learned about my rights and how to advocate for myself and others. With the confidence I
gained, I started volunteering with SRI, helping others in my community. Today, I’m proud to say that
I’ve become a leader in our refugee community, working alongside SRI to support others in their
journeys."
