Why Your Support Matters

In the face of adversity, refugees and displaced persons are often left without the basic necessities of
life. They face uncertain futures, lacking the stability and support needed to rebuild their lives.
SRI stands as a beacon of hope, working relentlessly to bridge the gap between despair and opportunity.
But we cannot do it alone. We need your help to make a profound impact.

Be the Change: Support Refugees Initiative (SRI)

Imagine a world where every voice is heard, every right is respected, and every person has the
chance to thrive.

Are you passionate about creating a better future for refugees and internally displaced persons?

At the Support Refugees Initiative (SRI), we're on a mission to ensure that the voices of refugees are
heard, their rights are respected, and they have access to essential social services. Our vision is a
world where every individual, whether a refugee or a national, can aspire to a brighter, more secure
future.

What We Do:

  • Advocacy & Protection
  • Education
  • Livelihoods
  • Nutrition & Medical Care
  • WASH (Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene)
  • Psychosocial Support
