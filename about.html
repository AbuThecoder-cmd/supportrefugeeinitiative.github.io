Support Refugees Initiative (SRI): Empowering Lives, Building Futures

Origin:

Founded with a vision to address the urgent needs of refugees and internally displaced persons, the
Support Refugees Initiative (SRI) stands as a beacon of hope and empowerment. Established to
tackle the challenges faced by marginalized communities, we operate with unwavering commitment
to improve lives and foster a more inclusive world.

Together, we can create a world where hope shines brightly, and every individual has the chance
to thrive .

Mission: To ensure that the voices of refugees are heard, their rights are respected and
they have access to social services.
Objectives of SRI.
  1. Ensuring peace of mind in the refugee communities.
  2. Creating assimilation between refugees and nationals.
  3. Strengthening the livelihood of the refugee communities.
  4. Ending GBV in both nationals and refugees.
  5. Ensuring proper up bringing of the younger generation both in refugees and
    nationals.
  6. Ensuring protect of humanity in areas of instabilities.
Words of Wisdom for Donors:

"Empowerment and hope are the twin beacons that guide us through the darkest times.
Your contribution to SRI is a gift that empowers individuals and families to overcome their challenges and
fosters hope for a better, more inclusive world. Together, we can create lasting change and build a
future where everyone has the opportunity to succeed."
