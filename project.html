Support Refugees Initiative

Explanation:
The Support Refugees Initiative (SRI) is dedicated to empowering the lives of refugees who have
been uprooted from their homes due to conflict, persecution, or natural disasters. By providing
access to education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihood opportunities, we are not just addressing
immediate needs but also paving the way for long-term stability and independence. Empowerment
is about restoring dignity and hope, enabling refugees to rebuild their lives with confidence and
resilience.
