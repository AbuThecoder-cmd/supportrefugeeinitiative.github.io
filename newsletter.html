Our Services

Feeding the Future: Nourishment for Refugees

At SRI, we believe that food is a fundamental human right and a critical first step towards rebuilding lives. Through our food distribution programs, we provide refugees with nutritious meals that not only sustain them physically but also offer comfort and hope in times of uncertainty.

Healing Hands: Medical Aid for Refugees

Health is a cornerstone of dignity and resilience. SRI's medical aid initiatives ensure that refugees have access to the healthcare services they desperately need. Our dedicated medical teams work tirelessly to heal wounds, treat illnesses, and promote overall well-being.

Spreading Knowledge: Sensitization for Refugees

Empowerment begins with education. SRI's sensitization programs equip refugees with vital information about their rights, health, and community resources. Through workshops, seminars, and one-on-one counseling, we help refugees understand their circumstances and make informed decisions.

A Light in the Darkness: Giving Hope to Refugees

Hope is the spark that ignites change. At SRI, we strive to keep that spark alive by offering emotional and psychological support to refugees. Our programs are designed to uplift spirits, build resilience, and encourage a positive outlook on the future.

Dressing with Dignity: Providing Clothing to Refugees

Clothing is more than just fabric; it is a symbol of dignity and protection. SRI’s clothing distribution efforts ensure that refugees have access to suitable attire for all seasons and circumstances. By providing clean, comfortable clothing, we help restore a sense of normalcy and self-respect.

Championing Justice: Supporting Refugee Rights

Every refugee has the right to live with dignity, security, and freedom. SRI is committed to advocating for the rights of refugees at every level. We provide legal assistance, campaign for policy changes, and raise awareness about the challenges refugees face.

Sheltering Lives: Providing Safe Haven for Refugees

A safe place to rest and recover is essential for any displaced person. SRI is dedicated to providing secure, stable housing solutions for refugees, whether through temporary shelters, long-term housing programs, or assistance in finding permanent homes.

Empowering Futures: Education and Skills Training for Refugees

Education is the key to unlocking potential and fostering independence. SRI’s educational programs offer refugees of all ages the opportunity to learn, grow, and build the skills necessary for a successful future.

Building Community: Fostering Integration and Solidarity

Community is at the heart of SRI’s mission. We believe that integration and social cohesion are vital for the successful resettlement of refugees. Our programs are designed to bring refugees and host communities together, fostering mutual understanding, respect, and cooperation.

Protecting the Vulnerable: Safeguarding Refugee Women and Children

Women and children are often the most vulnerable in times of crisis. SRI is dedicated to protecting their rights and ensuring their safety. We provide specialized support services for women and children, including safe spaces, counseling, and educational programs.

Advancing Equality: Promoting Gender and Social Inclusion

SRI is committed to advancing gender equality and social inclusion within refugee communities. We work to ensure that all refugees, regardless of gender, age, or background, have equal access to resources, opportunities, and services.
