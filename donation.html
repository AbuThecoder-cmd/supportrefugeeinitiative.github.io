why your donation can impact not only one person but the entire community of the refugees in kyanguali.

Here are the benefits of donating to the Support Refugees Initiative, categorized by
the impact on hope, rights, and voice:

Giving Them Hope

  • Restoring Dignity:Your donation helps provide essential resources such as food, shelter, and
    healthcare, giving refugees the hope and strength to rebuild their lives.
  • Empowering Futures:By supporting educational programs, you give refugees, especially
    children, the hope for a brighter future and the tools to break the cycle of poverty.

Supporting Their Rights

  • Legal Protection: Donations help ensure refugees receive legal assistance to safeguard their
    rights, such as protection from discrimination, access to justice, and the right to asylum.
  • Access to Social Services: Your contribution helps refugees access essential services like
    healthcare and education, reinforcing their rights to live with dignity and security.

Making Their Voice Heard

  • Advocacy and Representation: Donations fund advocacy efforts to ensure that the voices of
    refugees are heard at local, national, and international levels, influencing policies that affect their lives.
  • Community Engagement: By supporting initiatives that engage refugees in decision-making
    processes, you help amplify their voices, ensuring they have a say in the issues that matter to them.
